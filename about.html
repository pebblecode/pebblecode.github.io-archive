OUR CULTURE

Since inception, pebble {code} has strived to create an environment in which the best designers, developers and creative technologists can thrive. Running regular hack days, encouraging technology exploration, giving time for personal development and having a results only work culture has meant that we attract the best talent.

Founded in 2010 by George, Alex and Toby, pebble {code} has enjoyed success in Financial Services, Pharmaceutical, Broadcasting & Gaming. Our founders saw modern technology moving so rapidly and big business struggling to utilise it, and wanted to bridge the gap.

With prior experience of big business architecture and more traditional application development methods, pebble {code} can help transform organisations into technically innovative companies able to stay ahead of their competition.
